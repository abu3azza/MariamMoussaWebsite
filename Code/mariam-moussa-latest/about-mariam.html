Biography

Mariam is a Certified Professional Co-Active Coach who is passionate to live purposefully and appreciates the privilege of coaching others and witnessing their magnificent growth and transformation.

Mariam has graduated from The American university in Cairo with High Honors, majoring in Economics and minoring in Business Administration and Architectural Engineering. Following graduation, Mariam has joined the Management Training Program at the multinational company, Heineken in Egypt and then worked as a Business Analyst in the Planning and Control Department.

Although Mariam was excelling at her job, there was still a part missing for her. Seeking fulfillment and more out of life, Mariam has decided to follow her heart desires regardless of what it takes and further expand her leadership, determination, professionalism, compassion, understanding and willingness to help other human beings by pursuing her dream, a passionate job that influences others and helps them to blossom, live fully and choose their own life story.

That brought Mariam to being a Tutor for children with learning disabilities at the Learning Resource Center (LRC) in Egypt since June 2014 and then her Coaching Career that started with the Coaching Training in London in early 2015 at the Coaches Training Institute (CTI) .

Mariam has been awarded her designation of Certified Professional Co-Active Coach (CPCC) on May 25th, 2016. Mariam has then achieved a certificate of Achievement by CRR Global for successfully completing the ORSC Fundamentals Module for Organisation & Relationship Systems Coaching on June 26th, 2016. In addition, The International Coach Federation (ICF) has awarded Mariam the designation of Associate Certified Coach (ACC) on July 7th, 2016.

The CTI coaching curriculum is accredited by the International Coach Federation (ICF) , approved by the California Board of Behavior Sciences (BBS), licensed by the Minnesota Office of Higher Education, approved by the Human Resources Professionals Association (HRPA), recommended for college credit by the American Council on Education (ACE), and awarded an European Quality Award (EQA) by the European Mentoring and Coaching Council (EMCC).

Mariam uses the Co-Active Model and offers her coaching in both, the English and the Arabic, languages to empower and transform the lives of her clients in Egypt and outside of Egypt.

Mariam’s Life Purpose is to be the bright star that breaks through with hope by opening up new perspectives.

As a Coach, Mariam is known for her strength, courage, passion, gentle determination, understanding, compassion, warmth and sense of purpose.

Mariam loves working with clients who are:

  • Living Life with Meaning & Purpose
  • Pursuing Dreams
  • Career Shifting
  • Entrepreneurs
  • Passionate Leaders
  • Aspiring Young Adults
  • Enhancing Relationships
  • Seeking Spirituality
  • And anyone who wants to take charge of writing their own life story!
