Start Coaching

BOOK a FREE SAMPLE SESSION

To start any personal coaching relationship, a free sample session is conducted. This is to ensure that the coachees paints a full picture of the process, tastes the impact of coaching and for the coach as well as the coachees to assess the compatibility of both ends in the relationship.

To start any professional coaching relationship, an introductory meeting is set with the hiring entity. This meeting aims to designing the alliance between the two ends of the coaching relationship and to reach a consensus of what, why, how, when, and where of the coaching process and its relative logistics.

At the end of the sample session or the introductory meeting, the coach and coachees or hiring entity agree and confirm to the terms of working together.
