What is coaching?

The International Coaching Federation (ICF) defines coaching as partnering with clients in a thought-provoking and creative process that inspires them to maximize their personal and professional potential, ... Read More

Is coaching right for you?

In a nutshell, do you or your company have a fairly clear idea of a desired outcome from the coaching? If yes, then a coaching partnership can be a useful tool for developing a strategy for how to achieve that outcome with greater ease and support. Try this out and see if coaching is for you!

What are some typical topics to work with a coach on?

ANYTHING! Change, transformation, development, work, studies, career shifting, entrepreneurship, leadership, relationships, family, recreation, self-awareness, habits or traits, perspectives on topics, self-confidence, transitioning, life purpose/meaning, spirituality, passion, dreams, values, etc…
